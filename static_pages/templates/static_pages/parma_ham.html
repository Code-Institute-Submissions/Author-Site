It’s all new, everything, even you. You only know that you have to get out, have to keep moving, and have to survive. Luckily fate has provided you with a (rather grumpy) friend in the form of a giant talking bear.

Together you explore, find friends, and begin to understand the world and each other. The shadow behind you is racing to catch up though, and secrets from your past dog your footsteps. If you want to keep your loved ones safe you must face your demons.

“Parma Ham & Little Blue was another idea that started off on holiday. My partner and I were in Japan, and I was keeping a diary. I like to do these little sketches of all the funny things that happen and because he’s quite a big hairy viking chap I was drawing him as a bear. These cartoons I was drawing in my diary were hilarious, this giant bear interacting with things in japan that were clearly built for someone much smaller. The story itself has nothing to do with us, that sort of grew as I was sketching, but the characters came from those early sketches.

- Holly Thomas
